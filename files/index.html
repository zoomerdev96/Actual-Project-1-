Experience the Magic

The premier Lepidoptera source in Western Massachusetts, get up close and personal to nearly 4,000 exotic and domestic butterflies in a tropical environment. Featuring an 8,000-square foot indoor conservatory, along with a wide variety of other insects and animal life, Magic Wings focuses on butterfly-related education, recreation, entertainment, and gardening needs.

Committed to Our Founding Mission

Opened in October of 2000 by George and Sharon Miller, Magic Wings is an 18,400-square foot facility dedicated to the propogation and preservation of butterflies both foreign and domestic. Magic Wings is the foremost butterfly conservatory in Massachusetts, and prides itself on striving to fulfill its ongoing mission of educational outreach and raising awareness for the conservation of butterfly habitats as well as the environment as a whole.

Although George and Sharon are no longer with us, they will forever remain foremost in our hearts as we strive to further their goals of education and outreach through the operation and expansion of the conservatory they founded.
